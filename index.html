What We Do

We repair all types of Personal, Office or Home Equipment and Appliances. Our rates are surprisingly very minimal and only genuine parts are used.

Computers

We repair Desktops and Laptops, we fix hardware problems like motherboard damages, screen replacement, keyboard replacement, upgrading, hinge repair, CDRom Drive repair and software problems like connectivity issues, antivirus installation, compatibility issues and installation of professional software.

Office Equipment

We undertake the repairs of all equipment that are office related like Servers, Computers, Printers, Photocopiers, Shredders, Projectors, Inverters, Air-Conditioners, Biometric systems, Surveillance systems, Table phones, Radios (commercial and professional), E-Presence systems, and Networking equipment.

Home Appliances

We repair home appliances like Flat Screen Tvs, Fridges, Generators, Home theatre systems, Electric ovens, Steam Iron, Washing machines, Microwave Oven and Fans.

How We Do It

We diagnose and repair without collecting your money, you are only asked to pay AFTER we have successfully repaired your equipment.

01

Each equipment or item repair must be duly authorized by the owner or someone appointed by him or her, this will be the primary contact person whose phone number we will use for communication. We have a simple and straightforward process that helps to get the job done right.

02

The problem or faults of the said equipment will be discussed with you and repair options will be clearly outlined for you to choose from.
Each installation will be based on the guidelines of the manufacturer, if your equipment is still under warranty, we will advice you appropriately.

03

Cost of each repair will be discussed and authorization obtained before any repairs are carried out on your equipment. If we are unable to fix the equipment due irreparable damage, we will return the equipment to you exactly as it came and you will not be charged anything.
